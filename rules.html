Rules and Regulations

Last Updated: March 2025

Welcome to CodeCracked! To ensure a productive and respectful learning environment, all users must adhere to the following rules and regulations. By accessing and using this platform, you agree to comply with these guidelines.

1. General Conduct

  • Users must engage respectfully with other learners and instructors.
  • Any form of harassment, hate speech, or offensive language will not be tolerated.
  • Spamming, advertising, or promoting unrelated content is strictly prohibited.

2. Account Usage

  • Each user is responsible for maintaining the security of their account credentials.
  • Sharing accounts or using another person’s account is not allowed.
  • Any suspicious activity or unauthorized access should be reported immediately.

3. Learning Materials & Content

  • All lessons, quizzes, and materials provided on the platform are for educational purposes only.
  • Users may not copy, redistribute, or sell any website content without permission.
  • Code examples and exercises should be used ethically and within fair learning practices.

4. Plagiarism & Cheating

  • Users should complete quizzes, exercises, and assignments honestly.
  • Any form of plagiarism, including copying answers from external sources, will result in penalties.
  • Collaboration is encouraged, but submitting another user’s work as your own is strictly prohibited.

5. Community & Forums

  • Discussions should remain constructive and relevant to web development topics.
  • Users must not post inappropriate, misleading, or harmful content.
  • Any violation of community guidelines may lead to suspension or banning from forums.

6. Technical Usage & Restrictions

  • Attempting to hack, manipulate, or disrupt the platform is strictly forbidden.
  • Automated bots or scripts must not be used to access website features.
  • Any detected security breaches may result in legal action.

7. Reporting Violations

If you notice a violation of these rules, please report it immediately via our support channels.

CodeCracked reserves the right to review, investigate, and take appropriate action against any misconduct.

8. Amendments & Updates

These rules and regulations may be updated periodically.

Continued use of the platform signifies agreement with the latest version of these rules.

By following these guidelines, we can create a respectful and engaging learning environment for all users. Thank you for being a part of CodeCracked!
